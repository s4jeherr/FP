W1 flood
Date: January 4, 2022 at 2:00 p.m.
Alarm type: FME
Duration: 45 minutes
Type of use: storm situation
Place of use: Gutweiler / Gusterath-Valley
Vehicles: MZF 2
More forces: fire brigade Gutweiler, Korlingen fire brigade, fire brigade Sommerau

Operation report:
At noon we were alerted to Gusterath-Valley to support the Gutweiler, Korlingen and Sommerau fire brigades.

Due to the continuing rain, the Ruwer level rose to such an extent that sandbags had to be laid as a precaution to protect neighboring houses.

With our multi -purpose vehicle we transported the required sandbags from our warehouse to the site.
