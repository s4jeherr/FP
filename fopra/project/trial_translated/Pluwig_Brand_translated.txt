B2.08 apartment fire
Date: April 19, 2024 at 12:31 p.m.
Alarm type: DME, siren
Duration: 1 hour 24 minutes
Type of use: building fire
Place of use: Schöndorf
Team strength: 17
Vehicles: MLF, TLF 16/24-TR, TSF, MTW 1
More forces: Fire Brigade Schöndorf, Fire Brigade Waldrach, FEZ Ruwer, emergency services, WL Ruwer

Operation report:
On Friday afternoon we were alerted to a building fire in Schöndorf.

A shelter on a residential building had caught fire. The fire had already spread to the building facade when the fire brigade arrived.

The fire could quickly be brought under control by the fire brigade.
