B3 - container fire on buildings
Date: May 30, 2024 at 0:03 a.m.
Duration: 20 minutes
Type of use: fire use 
Place of use: Güdingen, Saargemünder Straße
Operations manager: OBM Lergon
Team strength: 1/16
Units and vehicles:

Saarbrücken professional fire brigade
HLF 23/46-1, HLF 23/46-2
Operation report:
At midnight, the control center was reported to a burning container, which was directly on a building. On arrival, a heavily smoking rubble container on the old primary school building could be found.


A group of respiratory protection made the quick attack and extinguished the fire. In order to reach the deeper embers, foam was used and the container flooded with it. After about 30 minutes, fire could be reported.


At the fire brigade godface, the troop used was supplied with fresh operating clothing and the HLF with new respiratory protection devices by the site hygiene. End.

The police took up the investigation in all operations, as there is a reasonable suspicion of arson.
