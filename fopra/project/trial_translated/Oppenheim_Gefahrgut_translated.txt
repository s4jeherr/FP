Expiring operating substances
15.05.2024, 10:50 a.m.
Oppenheim - A technical defect in a garbage vehicle led to an operation by the fire brigade in the Oppenheim suburb at 10:50 a.m. Hydraulic oil escaped on the vehicle. After the fire brigade was stopped, the site could be handed over to the crew of the garbage vehicle, which took care of further measures. The Kautzbrunnenweg had to be closed briefly for the duration of the measures.
