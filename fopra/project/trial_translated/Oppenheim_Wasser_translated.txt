Personal rescue water
11/23/2024, 5:20 p.m.
Oppenheim / Gimbsheim - At 5:20 p.m., the fire brigade was supported for a water rescue operation on the Rhine between Guntersblum and Gimbsheim. According to the reporting, one person should be up to their shoulders in the water. After a short exploration, a person could be found on a gray, which was recorded and brought on land by the GunterSblum fire brigade. The multi -purpose boat was then able to end the operation. The operational control car and team transport car were also able to leave the previously risen provision room in Guntersblum.
