Operation report:
In the early Friday morning there was a dangerous property accident on one of the loading lamps of a Güdingen freight forwarder. On site, a 200 liter of barrel, filled with a dangerous goods and completely leaked.


After an emergency colony was set up, exploration under respiratory protection was carried out. The danger and the extent of the damage were assessed as checked and the measures are determined. The application of a binder was sufficient to prevent damage. There were no further dangers, so that a disposal company can now take care of a professional disposal. Measures carried out also remained in areas of harmless values. Nobody was injured in the operation. The site was handed over to the management.
