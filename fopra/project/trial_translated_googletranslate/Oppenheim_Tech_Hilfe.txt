Door opening / false alarm
March 10, 2024, 6:43 p.m.
OPPENHEIM - At 6:43 p.m., the fire department was alerted to a door being opened. The people who called were worried because they had not been able to reach their relatives. After ringing and knocking several times, the alleged victim opened the door himself safely. The fire department was able to leave without taking any further action.
