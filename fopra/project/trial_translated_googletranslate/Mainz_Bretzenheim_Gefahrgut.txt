Alert: September 23, 2024 - 6:04 p.m.
Type of operation: G2
Keyword: ICT: hazardous materials operation
Vehicles: MTF, ELW 2
Operation report:
The ICT unit was called to a hazardous materials operation in Mainz-Gonsenheim to provide command support with the ELW2 and our MTF. A chemical reaction with heat and smoke development had occurred on site in an industrial plant.
