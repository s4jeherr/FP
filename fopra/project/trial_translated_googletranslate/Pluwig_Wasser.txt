W1 flood
Date: January 4, 2022 at 2:00 p.m.
Type of alarm: FME
Duration: 45 minutes
Type of operation: severe weather
Location: Gutweiler / Gusterath-Tal
Vehicles: MZF 2
Other forces: Gutweiler fire department, Korlingen fire department, Sommerau fire department

Operation report:
At noon we were alerted to Gusterath-Tal to support the Gutweiler, Korlingen and Sommerau fire departments.

Due to the persistent rain, the level of the Ruwer rose so much that sandbags had to be laid as a precaution to protect neighboring residential buildings.

With our multi-purpose vehicle we transported the required sandbags from our warehouse to the site.
