B2.08 Apartment fire
Date: April 19, 2024 at 12:31 p.m.
Type of alarm: DME, siren
Duration: 1 hour 24 minutes
Type of operation: Building fire
Location: Schöndorf
Crew size: 17
Vehicles: MLF, TLF 16/24-Tr, TSF, MTW 1
Other forces: Schöndorf fire department, Waldrach fire department, FEZ Ruwer, rescue service, WL Ruwer

Operation report:
On Friday afternoon we were alerted to a building fire in Schöndorf.

A shelter in a residential building had caught fire. The fire had already spread to the building facade when the fire department arrived.

The fire department was able to quickly bring the fire under control.
