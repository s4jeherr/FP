Rescue of people in water
11/23/2024, 5:20 p.m.
Oppenheim / GIMBSHEIM - At 5:20 p.m., the fire department was called to assist in a water rescue operation on the Rhine between Guntersblum and Gimbsheim. According to the person reporting, a person was in the water up to their shoulders. After a brief investigation, a person was found on a jetty, who was picked up by the Guntersblum fire department's rescue boat and brought to shore. The multi-purpose boat was then able to end the operation. The command vehicle and personnel transport vehicle were also able to leave the staging area in Guntersblum that they had previously arrived at.
